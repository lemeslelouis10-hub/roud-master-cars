ROAD MASTER CARS Privacy Policy

This policy applies to the ROAD MASTER CARS Android app, an offline vehicle rental catalog and local cost estimator.

Effective date: 2026-07-07

Offline App Catalog data is stored locally inside the app.
No Tracking No analytics, ads, tracking SDKs, or login systems.
No Permissions The app does not request device permissions.

Summary

  • The app is an offline vehicle rental catalog and local cost estimator.
  • The app does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.
  • There are no ads, analytics, tracking SDKs, or login systems.
  • The app does not request device permissions.
  • The app does not use Android backup for app data.
  • All catalog data is stored locally inside the app.

Data We Collect

None. ROAD MASTER CARS does not collect personal data through this app.

Local App Data

Vehicle listings, company information, FAQ text, legal text, and indicative prices are bundled with the app and displayed locally on your device.

The app does not create user accounts or send app data to ROAD MASTER CARS or any third party.

Actions You Choose

If you tap Share, Android opens another app on your device. Any information you choose to send outside this app is handled by that external app or service.

Third-Party Services

Sharing apps are separate services. Their own privacy policies apply to any data you send through them.
